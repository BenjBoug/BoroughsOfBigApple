Staten Island

Presentation

Staten Island is both an island (Staten Island) and one of the five boroughs of New York City in the United States. It coincides with the County of Richmond. In 2010, its population was 468,730 inhabitants. The Staten Island is located at the opposite of southern of Manhattan, across the main basin of the port. The area of Staten Island is 265 km2 (151 km2 of land are emerging). Its highest point is Hill Todt at 125 meters. This is also the highlight for all of New York. Staten Island is connected to the Mainland (New Jersey), the Bayonne Bridge, Goethals Bridge and Outerbridge Crossing Bridge, and Brooklyn by the Verrazano-Narrows Bridge. Staten Island is clearly divided into two, North Shore, north of the Staten Island Expressway and South Shore, south of the highway. As district of New York, Staten Island also includes the island of Staten Island, but also five other small unhabited islands: Isle of Meadow(Fresh Kills in the river), Isle Pralle (in the Arthur Kill River), Shooters Island (in Newark Bay), Swinburne Island (in New York Bay) , and Hoffman Island (in New York Bay) .
