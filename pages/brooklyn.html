Brooklyn

Presentation

Brooklyn is one of five boroughs of New York City in the United States. It coincides with the Kings County. With a population of over 2,504,700 inhabitants in 2010, is one who has the largest population, it would constitute on its own the fourth city of the United State. The area of Brooklyn is 251 km2, 183 km2 of land. Its highest point is around Prospect Park and Green-Wood Cemetery, about 61 meters above sea level The Brooklyn Heights in the city center is built on a hill.

The borough of Brooklyn occupies the western end of Long Island and is adjacent to the Queens district. Its northern coast is defined by the East River, which separatesManhattan. Brooklyn is connected since 1883 by the Brooklyn Bridge, which is the longest suspension bridge in the United States. Other bridges were built later, including the Williamsburg Bridge and Manhattan Bridge. At the South-west, we can find Gowanus Gowanus Canal and Bay. At its western end, Brooklyn is separated from Staten Island by the Narrows, where Upper and Lower meet New-York Bay. The Verrazano-Narrows Bridge, opened in 1964 to link the two boroughs. It was from 1964 to 1981 the longest suspension bridge in the world. South-east are ConeyIsland, Brighton Beach, Manhattan Beach and Jamaica Bay.
